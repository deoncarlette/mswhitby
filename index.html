About Me

Hi! I’m Ms. Whitby, and I teach Computer Science here at Wagner High School. I’m passionate about making technology engaging, relevant, and accessible to every student, no matter their starting point.

Before teaching, I worked in tech and have a background in law, which means I bring a unique perspective to problem-solving and project design. I believe coding is more than just writing lines of code—it’s a creative tool for building real-world solutions, telling stories, and even having a little fun along the way.

Classroom Expectations

  • ▶No Cell Phones!
  • ▶Respect – Treat yourself, classmates, and equipment with care.
  • ▶Engage & Try – Participation matters; mistakes are part of learning.
  • ▶Collaborate – Work together and share ideas—tech is a team sport.
  • ▶Stay Curious – Ask questions and explore beyond the lesson.
  • ▶Integrity – Do your own work; give credit where it’s due.
